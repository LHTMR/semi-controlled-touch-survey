## Welcome

#### **Q1**  |  `Welcome`  |  *Display / instruction text*

Welcome to the online survey of social touch!

By participating in this survey, you are helping us to understand social touch, and the link between how it is performed and how it is interpreted.

The survey takes around 3-10 minutes, and we will ask you to do the following:

1. Provide some basic information

2. Watch a short video clip of social touch

3. Describe the touch in different ways

4. Answer some questions regarding the touch you described

5. Optionally repeat steps 2-4 with new videos.
